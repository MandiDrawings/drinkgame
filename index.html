A LONG TIME AGO, IN A GALAXY FAR, FAR AWAY...

THE SACRED LEMON has been intercepted during transit to the Free Cocktail Systems. An unknown Imperial faction has disrupted all official channels and erased its trajectory from the archives. The Republic denies any involvement. What remains is chaos… and a single surviving trace.

A small Force-sensitive being has left a fragmented transmission: GROGU.

“Danger… sacred citrus lost…”
“Message hidden at the arrival point of secrets…”
“The mailbox holds the first clue…”
“Do not delay… the Empire is listening…”

Before the signal fades completely, Grogu embedded a security override.

ACCESS CODE: CITRON-OVERRIDE

Use it to unlock the Imperial pathway and continue the mission. May the Force guide your cocktail.
